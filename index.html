A great player, A Great Human Being

Leo has been involved in humanitarian initiatives targeted at helping disadvantaged children throughout his career, a devotion that originates in part from the medical issues he endured as a child. Leo was named a UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador in March 2010. He travelled to Haiti with UNICEF shortly after his appointment to raise public awareness about the condition of the country's children in the aftermath of the earthquake. Leo Messi formed his own philanthropic organisation, the Leo Messi Foundation, which invests in health-care services to better the lives of children, in addition to his work with UNICEF.
